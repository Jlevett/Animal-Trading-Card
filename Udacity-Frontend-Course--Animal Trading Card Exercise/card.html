Sloth

The sloth is so slow, that it is actually named after its slowness, as the meaning of sloth is laziness or sluggishness.

  • Scientific Name: Folivora
  • Average Length: 42-80 cm
  • Average LifeSpan: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Tropical trees

Sloths prefer sleeping while curled into a ball in the fork of a tropical tree. They also like to sleep hanging by their claws from tree branches. For the most part, a sloth's life revolves around sleeping and eating in their tree homes. The only times these mammals leave their tree is to use the bathroom and to take a swim.
